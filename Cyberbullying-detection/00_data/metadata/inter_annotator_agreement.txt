## Confusion Patterns

### Most Common Disagreements

1. harassment ↔ aggression (47 cases)
   - Root cause: Overlap in hostile behavior
   - Resolution: Harassment requires repetition/pattern

2. stalking ↔ cyberstalking (34 cases)
   - Root cause: Online vs offline distinction unclear
   - Resolution: Cyberstalking specifically digital

3. toxicity ↔ insult (29 cases)
   - Root cause: Personal vs environmental negativity
   - Resolution: Toxicity = general, Insult = targeted

4. insult ↔ hate (23 cases)
   - Root cause: Individual vs group targeting
   - Resolution: Hate requires group identity basis

5. harassment ↔ stalking (21 cases)
   - Root cause: Monitoring vs general unwanted contact
   - Resolution: Stalking = observation/tracking focus
